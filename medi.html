In this article I will explain the methods and the daily life exercises for learning to do Meditation.

First of all If You have a mindset that Meditation is used to stop the mind from thinking,Your mindset is wrong.
The more focus and energy You put in stopping Your thoughts and shutting your mind the more thoughts will come.

Instead of focusing on thoughts, focus on things that are in continous motion

[+]Environment

You see Meditation requires all your energy to be focused on one thing,that is the reason why Environment matters here.
Try to find silent place like a garden or seashore.
When You get good at silent places You can move to any place that You like but in start You have to with silent places

[+]Time

Meditation doesn't requires You to be Punctual of time, You can do it anytime when Your mind is stress free.

[+]meditation Music

No Dont try it, You have to do meditation without any relaxing music or anything,You will train better.

[+]method 1

When You begin to do meditation just focus the conciousness on your belly which is in continous motion
look at its expansion and contraction with mind's eye

[+] method 2

In this method You have to walk and focus the awarness on your feets and legs this method is great for begginers who find it tough to
focus on breath or heartbeat

[+] method 3

This method is very simple just you have to do is lay down or sit down and just focus on your breath
