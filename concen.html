Concentration Exercise

Before We begin the exercise let me remind You that Concentration can cause headache
Strain and Pain in eyes. So stop the exercise when You feel any problem with it.
The key is to concentrate as for much as You can without making yourself uncomfortable.

Note: Repeat this exercise a minimum of two times and a maximum of three times a day

Exercise

Before You begin make sure to meditate for 1 or 2 minutes to relax your mind.
Make sure no one disturbs You during this exercise.

You can also try this enter time in minutes or seconds and You are good to go.
After You have done You can meditate to improve.

The other method is this....

1) Take a white blank paper.

2) Make a black dot in the middle most of the paper.

3) Adjust it in front of your chest.

4) If you feel any kind of pressure in Your eyes
increase or decrease the vertical position until You get comfortable.

5) Try to reach 15 minutes but don't push yourself too hard
Listen to yourself also.

Tips:

When You concentrate, slowly and slowly lose consciousness about
everything yourself, your body, your environment.

When You lose Consciousness you will not be able to feel
Your body and it will be like You are numb.

When You concentrate just feel the dot
feel its taste feel its smell feel the feeling of touch on it, Capture everything about it.
